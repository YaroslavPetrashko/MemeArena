--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT MEMEARENA IS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MemeArena is an onchain crypto-meme card battler inspired by Marvel SNAP. Players
build a deck of internet-meme characters, fight across a 3-location board, and
win by controlling 2 of 3 lanes. The tone is funny, fast, degen-crypto-native,
and entertainment-first — a real game, not a financial product.

Tagline energy: "Play for fun. Bonk responsibly."

World premise: Meme characters from crypto/internet culture clash in surreal
arenas — neon cosmic battlefields, liminal backrooms, Miami pump zones, and
degen trading dens.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VISUAL STYLE GUIDE (for all generated art)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overall aesthetic:
  - Premium mobile card-game art (Marvel SNAP, Clash Royale quality bar)
  - Dark cinematic battle scenes with neon accents
  - Crypto-degen humor without being grim or realistic
  - Bold, readable silhouettes; characters should read at small card size
  - Saturated colors, high contrast, slight glow/bloom on accents

Brand palette:
  - Primary green:   #2fd573 (UI accent, positive actions)
  - Lime neon:       #b6ff1b (playable glow, prismatic tier, battle embers)
  - Magenta neon:    #ff2bd6 (cosmic highlights, prismatic tier secondary)
  - Gold:            #ffd24a (rewards, gold frame tier, apex rank)
  - Dark base:       #050308 to #07070b (battle backgrounds, deep space)
  - Surface:         #0d0d14 (UI panels)

Battle scene mood:
  - Full-screen cosmic arena: deep black/purple void, drifting nebula washes
    in lime + magenta, faint receding dot-grid floor, soft vignette
  - Floating ember particles in lime green
  - Locations sit as large rhombus/diamond-shaped panels (chamfered top/bottom)
  - Three locations side-by-side; player cards at bottom, enemy at top

Card art format (CHARACTERS):
  - Frameless full-art PNG — the entire card IS the image
  - Cost (Energy) and Power (Strength) are BAKED INTO the art (top corners
    or edges), like Marvel SNAP cards
  - Character name can be baked into bottom of card
  - Recommended size: 512×512 or 4:3 portrait, PNG with transparency OK
  - No rarity borders (rarity was removed from the game)
  - Character should dominate the frame; expressive, meme-accurate, slightly
    exaggerated for humor
  - Optional subtle glow matching ability type:
      Ongoing     → steady aura ring
      On Reveal   → burst/flash energy at reveal moment
      Conditional → situational shimmer (brighter when "winning")
      End Game    → ominous late-game purple/gold crackle

Location art format:
  - Full-art location card in rhombus/diamond silhouette
  - Location name + effect text baked into the image
  - Hexagonal card slots visible at top (enemy) and bottom (player) of the panel
  - Environment should tell the location's story at a glance
  - Wide enough to read as a horizontal panel (~16:9 or wider)

Cosmetic frame tiers (card upgrades — visual only, no stat change):
  Lv 1 Base       — no glow, plain
  Lv 2 Bronze     — warm copper ring #cd7f32
  Lv 3 Silver     — cool silver ring #cdd3da
  Lv 4 Gold       — gold ring #ffd24a
  Lv 5 Prismatic  — lime + magenta dual glow #b6ff1b / #ff2bd6

Competitive rank badges (for profile/leaderboard art, not cards):
  Paperhands → Shrimp → Crab → Degen → Ape → Chad → Whale → Giga → Meme Lord 👑

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAMEPLAY CONTEXT (for scene composition)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Match structure:
  - 6 turns, simultaneous reveal each turn
  - 3 locations revealed on turns 1, 2, and 3 (one new lane per turn)
  - Each location holds up to 4 cards per side (except Garage: 1 per side)
  - Players spend Energy (1→6 ramping each turn) to play cards from a 12-card deck
  - Win 2 of 3 locations by having higher total Power at match end
  - Cards flip face-down when played, then all reveal together at end of turn

Ability timing (useful for action-shot art):
  - On Reveal: triggers when the card flips face-up at turn end
  - Ongoing: passive aura while the card stays in play
  - Conditional: checks board state (e.g. "if winning this location")
  - End Game: triggers after turn 6 scoring

Board layout (top → bottom):
  [ Enemy score badges — 3 hexagons ]
  [ Enemy cards at each location — face-down or revealed ]
  [ LOCATION PANEL — environment art + effect text ]
  [ Player cards at each location ]
  [ Player hand — fanned cards at bottom ]
  [ Energy orb + End Turn button ]

Key UI elements to reference in promotional art:
  - Hexagonal score badges per location
  - Green energy orb (fills 1–6 over the match)
  - Angled "End Turn" button
  - Card hand fan with drag-to-place interaction

CONTENT NOTES FOR ARTISTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- No card rarity visuals — all cards are equal tier visually (only cosmetic
  upgrade frames differ: bronze/silver/gold/prismatic).
- Characters are internet memes; stay recognizable to the source meme while
  elevating to premium game-art quality.
- The battle screen is ALWAYS dark/cinematic even if the rest of the app uses
  light mode — location and card art should read well on dark backgrounds.
- Missing art degrades to gradient + initials — never generate art that relies
  on an external frame the code adds; the PNG is the whole card.
- Land Wolf's internal ID is "kekius_maximus" but the character depicted is
  Land Wolf (not a Roman emperor).
- Dogwifhat's file slug is "wif.png"; Chillguy's is "chill-guy.png".
- MemeArena is a game, not an investment product — avoid "guaranteed gains"
  imagery; humor and competition are the focus.
